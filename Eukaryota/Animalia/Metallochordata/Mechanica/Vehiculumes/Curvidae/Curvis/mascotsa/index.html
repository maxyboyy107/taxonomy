Curvis mascotsa Fictional Species

Curvis mascotsa (commonly known as the Common Curved Jet-engine) is a species of Jet-engine animal belonging to the family Curvidae. The species roams around and powers engines belonging to air vehicles by utilizing its unique curved jet propulsion system. The most famous of it's kind is "Sky Pod", a character in Air Canada kids books, according to them "Making flying more fun for kids".
