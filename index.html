About This Site

max's taxonomy is a homepage for both scientific classification and creative taxonomy. It brings together factual information about biological groups and fictional systems used in novels, games, and worldbuilding.

Real Taxonomy

Browse kingdoms, phyla, classes, orders, families, genera, and species. Understand how scientists categorize organisms from bacteria to mammals and how classification changes with new discoveries.

Fictional Taxonomy

I also have a section dedicated to fictional organisms like Pokémon, historical and fantasy creatures, video game characters, and cartoons.

Compare & Create

Compare real and fictional grouping systems, and use the ideas here to build your own taxonomy for characters, creatures, or ecosystems.

Get Started

Use the navigation to jump into the sections that interest you, or search for a taxon or creature name. Whether you're a student, writer, or worldbuilder, this site helps you connect patterns across reality and imagination.
